TeamSpeak 3 Server


I am hosting my own private TeamSpeak 3 Server that is free to use! Also I am available here when I am at my computer. Come message me or ask for or create a (temporary if you create it) private channel for your own use! It is available 24/7 so you can chat or game with your pals anytime you want!

You can connect to the server by typing ts3.gergely-szabo.com in the address field or using the new TeamSpeak feature called nicknames (Works with clients 3.1.6 and up)! With nicknames you can simply enter "gery" or "gergelyszabo" and hop in to the Lobby. Want even simpler? Click here to automatically join the server!
